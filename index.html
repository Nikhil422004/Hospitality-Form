General Information

  • Accommodation is arranged at external hotels.
  • Below mentioned rates are per day rates.
  • This form can be used for accommodation and transport.
  • Food stalls will be available in campus, participants can purchase food of their choice.
  • Transportation will be provided on chargeable basis from campus to hotel, if required.
  • Hotel Name: Orma Tourist Home
  • Location: Opposite Palakkad Jn Railway Station, Olavakkode, Palakkad (14kms from IIT Palakkad Nila Campus, Kanjikode)

Terms and Conditions

  • Rate per head :- 350/- for one day, 650/- for 2 days, 950/- for 3 days.
  • Rooms will be on sharing basis with other participants of the workshops.
  • If you are travelling in a group, fill names and make payment together in one single form for allocating rooms together.
  • Groups having boys and girls can fill the same form to avail accommodation in the same hotel. However, the boys and girls will split.
  • Check-Out time will be 24hrs from Check-in time if payment is done for one day. If exceeding 24hrs, payment for the next day has to be done.
  • Sharing is based on 3/4/5/7 and Dormitory.
  • Accommodation once booked cannot be cancelled & the amount will NOT be refunded.
  • ONLY the participants whose names are registered in this form will be allowed into the rooms. There will be an allocation list at the hotel for verifying the same.
  • Participants MUST carry a valid govt. ID card (aadhar card/ driving licence/ Ration card/ Voter Id etc... Pancard not accepted) for verification at the hotel.
  • If the room is not vacated before the corresponding check-out time, an additional amount for the next day has to be paid by the participants according to the hotel policies.
  • While checking out, your luggage should also be carried with you and the key must be handed over at the reception. Luggages should not be left at the hotel, and failing to submit the keys will lead to additional payment.
  • We do not recommend carrying anything expensive. Safety of your belongings is the responsibility of the participant.
  • Timings of all the workshops will be shared by respective organizers before the workshop. Participants are expected to reach the campus on their own before the event timings.
  • Transportation from Petrichor: Buses are available from hotels to campus in the morning and back to hotels after the workshops ends. Timings of the buses will be shared 2 days before the workshop.
